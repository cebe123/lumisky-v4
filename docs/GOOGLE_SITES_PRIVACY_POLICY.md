# Lumisky: Live Solar Wallpaper Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Adnan Cebe handles information in the Android application "Lumisky: Live Solar Wallpaper" (`com.lumisky.android`).

## 1. Developer and Privacy Contact

Developer: Adnan Cebe

Privacy contact: adnancebe@hotmail.com

## 2. What the App Does

Lumisky is a live wallpaper app that synchronizes wallpaper visuals with the local day, night, sun, and moon cycle. The app can use a manually selected city or, if the user grants permission, the device's current location.

## 3. Data Accessed, Used, or Stored

### Location Data

Lumisky may access approximate location (`ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`) and precise location (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`) only after the user grants Android runtime permission.

Location data is used for app functionality:

- calculating sunrise, sunset, solar noon, moon, and local daylight timing;
- synchronizing the live wallpaper with the user's local day/night cycle;
- showing a city or district label when available;
- keeping the wallpaper aligned when the user chooses current-location behavior.

The app can also be used with a manually selected city instead of current device location.

### Data Sent Off the Device

When online daylight synchronization is used, Lumisky may send latitude, longitude, and time zone information over HTTPS to `https://api.sunrise-sunset.org/json` to retrieve sunrise, sunset, and solar noon data. The app does not attach the user's name, email address, account, contact list, advertising identifier, or payment information to this request.

Android and Google services may also process data as part of their platform features:

- Google Play services location may provide location results after permission is granted.
- Android Geocoder may be used to resolve a location into a city or district label.
- Google Play In-App Review may be used if the user chooses or triggers the Play review flow; Lumisky does not receive the user's review text unless Google Play separately makes it available through Play Console.
- Android backup may back up local app preferences if the user has device backup enabled.

### Local App Data

Lumisky stores app settings locally on the device, including selected wallpaper, theme preferences, performance settings, selected/manual location, last known location data, and cached daylight results. This data is used to restore the selected wallpaper and app behavior.

## 4. Data Sharing

Lumisky does not sell personal or sensitive user data.

Lumisky does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or remote crash analytics SDKs.

Location coordinates and time zone information may be sent to the sunrise-sunset.org API for daylight calculation as described above. Platform services from Android, Google Play services, Google Play, and Android backup may process data according to the user's device settings and Google's applicable policies.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Local settings and cached daylight data remain on the device until the user changes settings, clears app storage, or uninstalls the app. Users can revoke location permission in Android system settings at any time.

Lumisky does not create user accounts. Because there is no Lumisky account, there is no account-deletion flow. For privacy questions or deletion requests related to direct email contact, users may contact adnancebe@hotmail.com.

## 6. Security

Network daylight requests use HTTPS. Local app settings are stored using Android app storage. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but Lumisky limits data use to the app functionality described in this policy.

## 7. Children

Lumisky is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app's data practices, permissions, or third-party services change. The "Last Updated" date will be changed when the policy is updated.

## 9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to: adnancebe@hotmail.com

## 3. Erişilen, Kullanılan veya Saklanan Veriler

### Konum Verileri

Lumisky, yaklaşık konuma (`ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`) ve hassas konuma (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`) yalnızca kullanıcı Android çalışma zamanı iznini verdikten sonra erişebilir.

Konum verisi uygulama işlevi için kullanılır:

- gündoğumu, günbatımı, güneşin tepe noktası, ay ve yerel gün ışığı saatlerini hesaplamak;
- canlı duvar kağıdını kullanıcının yerel gündüz/gece döngüsüyle senkronize etmek;
- mevcutsa şehir veya ilçe etiketini göstermek;
- kullanıcı mevcut konum davranışını seçtiğinde duvar kağıdını doğru yerel zamanlamayla güncel tutmak.

Uygulama, mevcut cihaz konumu yerine manuel seçilen bir şehirle de kullanılabilir.

### Cihaz Dışına Gönderilen Veriler

Çevrimiçi gün ışığı senkronizasyonu kullanıldığında Lumisky, gündoğumu, günbatımı ve güneşin tepe noktası verilerini almak için enlem, boylam ve zaman dilimi bilgisini HTTPS üzerinden `https://api.sunrise-sunset.org/json` adresine gönderebilir. Uygulama bu isteğe kullanıcının adını, e-posta adresini, hesabını, kişi listesini, reklam kimliğini veya ödeme bilgisini eklemez.

Android ve Google servisleri de platform özelliklerinin parçası olarak veri işleyebilir:

- Google Play services location, izin verildikten sonra konum sonucu sağlayabilir.
- Android Geocoder, bir konumu şehir veya ilçe etiketine dönüştürmek için kullanılabilir.
- Kullanıcı Play inceleme akışını seçerse veya tetiklerse Google Play In-App Review kullanılabilir; Lumisky, Google Play tarafından Play Console üzerinden ayrıca sunulmadıkça kullanıcının yorum metnini almaz.
- Kullanıcı cihaz yedeklemesini etkinleştirdiyse Android backup yerel uygulama tercihlerini yedekleyebilir.
